About The App

TREND Music App

The TREND Music App is a music player like iTunes while also having the functionality of shopping from a curated list of songs. To use the app, one must have an account and login using their credentials. The ability to create accounts is available and the information is automatically added to the database, thus letting the user log in without waiting.
